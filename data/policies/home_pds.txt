HOME AND CONTENTS INSURANCE — PRODUCT DISCLOSURE STATEMENT

SECTION 1 — ABOUT THIS POLICY
This policy covers your home building and contents against specified events. Issued by AAI Limited ABN 48 005 297 807.

SECTION 2 — BUILDING COVER

2.1 What Is Covered
Your home building is covered for sudden and accidental damage caused by:
- Fire, smoke, and explosion
- Storm, hail, lightning, and cyclone
- Flood (if selected as an optional add-on)
- Earthquake and tsunami
- Theft and attempted theft causing structural damage
- Impact from vehicles, aircraft, or falling trees
- Burst pipes and water damage from sudden leakage

2.2 Structural Elements
Building cover includes: foundations, floors, walls, ceilings, roof, fixed windows and doors, built-in wardrobes, kitchen cabinets, permanently installed air conditioning, solar panels, fences, gates, retaining walls, and driveways.

SECTION 3 — CONTENTS COVER

3.1 Standard Contents
Contents cover protects household goods and personal belongings against insured events when inside your home:
- Furniture, appliances, and electronics
- Clothing and personal effects
- Valuables up to $3,000 per item (higher limits require endorsement)
- Tools and equipment

3.2 Accidental Damage Add-On
Standard contents cover does not include accidental damage. The AccidentalDamage add-on must be selected at policy inception to cover:
- Carpets, rugs, and soft furnishings damaged accidentally
- Electronics damaged by accidental drops or spills
- Breakage of glass items

Without the AccidentalDamage add-on, only sudden damage caused by a listed event (storm, fire, theft) is covered for contents.

SECTION 4 — WATER DAMAGE

4.1 Sudden Water Damage
Sudden water damage is covered when caused by:
- Burst, leaking, or broken pipes
- Overflow of fixed appliances (washing machines, dishwashers)
- Storm-driven rain entering through damaged windows or roof

4.2 Gradual Damage Exclusion
We do not cover water damage caused by:
- Gradual leaks from pipes or fixtures (dripping taps, slow seepage)
- Rising damp, condensation, or mould
- Water entering through gaps in unsealed areas

4.3 Flexi Pipe Risk
Sub-standard flexible piping (flexi pipes) that fail over time are classified as gradual deterioration. Sudden catastrophic flexi pipe failure may be assessed case-by-case. We recommend replacing ageing flexi pipes to maintain full coverage eligibility.

SECTION 5 — FLOOD COVER

5.1 Definition of Flood
Flood means the covering of normally dry land by water that has escaped from the normal confines of any lake, river, creek, or other natural watercourse, whether or not resulting from rainfall.

5.2 Optional Flood Cover
Flood cover is an optional add-on and must be selected at policy inception. The annual premium for flood cover varies based on your property's assessed flood risk zone.

5.3 Storm vs Flood
If your property suffers water damage, we will determine whether the cause is storm (covered under standard policies) or flood (requires the add-on). You may request a written determination from our assessors.

SECTION 6 — EXCLUSIONS

6.1 General Exclusions
We do not cover:
- Damage caused intentionally by you or a household member
- Wear and tear, gradual deterioration, or structural defects
- Damage caused by pest infestation or vermin
- Sub-standard building materials or non-compliant workmanship
- Lithium-ion battery fires where the battery was non-certified or recalled

6.2 Liability Exclusions
We do not cover liability arising from intentional acts, business activities conducted from home without endorsement, or liability assumed under contract.

SECTION 7 — LIABILITY COVER

7.1 Legal Liability
This policy includes legal liability cover of up to $20 million for accidental bodily injury or property damage you cause to third parties as occupier of the insured property.

7.2 Notification Requirement
You must notify us within 48 hours of any incident that may give rise to a liability claim. Section 5.4: Failure to notify within 48 hours may affect your right to indemnity. Do not admit liability, make any payment, or enter any agreement without our written consent.

SECTION 8 — EXCESS AND CLAIMS

8.1 Standard Excess
The standard excess is $500 for building claims and $250 for contents claims.

8.2 Reduced Excess — Home Security
A 20% excess reduction applies where:
(a) An approved alarm system is installed and active
(b) All external doors and windows are fitted with approved locks
(c) The property is not left unoccupied for more than 60 consecutive days

8.3 Claim Process
Lodge all claims within 30 days of the event. Provide photographs, receipts or valuations for items over $500, and a statutory declaration for theft claims. Our assessors may inspect the property before approving large claims.
